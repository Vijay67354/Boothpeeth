Bodhpeeth: Global Expertise in Staffing, Skilling & Advisory Services

BodhPeeth is an emerging organization committed to empowering India’s youth and workforce through innovative solutions in Skill Development, Manpower Recruitment, and Payroll Management. Founded with a bridge the gap between industry demand and employability, BodhPeeth aims to create a sustainable ecosystem of learning, employment, and growth.

Our focus is to collaborate with government missions, corporate CSR initiatives, and private sector enterprises to deliver impactful skilling, livelihood, and human resource solutions that foster both individual and organizational success.


Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision

To become a trusted partner in nation-building by enabling skill excellence, employability, and workforce development across India.

Mission

• To design and implement training programs that enhance employability and promote self-reliance.

• To support industries with efficient recruitment and payroll management solutions.

• To build partnerships with government bodies, skill councils, and corporate organizations for impactful project delivery.

Core Values

• Integrity: We maintain transparency and honesty in every engagement.

• Innovation: We believe in evolving with technology and market needs.

• Commitment: We are dedicated to achieving measurable outcomes for every project.

• Excellence: We strive for quality in training, operations, and client satisfaction.

A. Skill Development

  • Design and implementation of demand-driven training programs aligned with NSQF and SSC standards.
  • Expertise in SIDH and SMART portals for project execution and compliance.
  • End-to-end management — candidate mobilization, training delivery, assessment, certification, and placement.
  • Focus on rural & semi-urban youth, women empowerment, and self-employment initiatives.

B. Manpower Recruitment

  • Providing skilled and semi-skilled manpower across healthcare, retail, logistics, and manufacturing.
  • Sourcing, screening, and deploying candidates as per client needs.
  • Specialized recruitment for project-based, contractual, and permanent staffing.

C. Payroll Management

  • Comprehensive payroll processing under EPF, ESI, TDS, and labor laws.
  • Customized payroll solutions for organizations of all sizes.
  • Ensuring accurate salary disbursement, statutory filing, and transparent reporting.
